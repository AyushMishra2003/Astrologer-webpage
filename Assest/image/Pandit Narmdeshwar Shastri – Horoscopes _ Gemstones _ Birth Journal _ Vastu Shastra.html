Know About Me

Pandit Narmdeshwar Shastri, having expertise in Vedic- Rishi tradition and knowledge tradition of Kashi, is, from a very young age, known for his accurate and successful prediction. Due to his uniqueness in Vedic knowledge, he has been honoured with a gold medal, given by none other than the Honourable Governor of Uttar Pradesh.

He completed his Masters from the Sanskrit University located in Varanasi, which is considered as a pioneer in nurturing ancient wisdom and Sanatan knowledge. From the beginning, Shastriji’s had deep allegiance and dedication towards ancient Indian sage tradition, which inspired him to discover the subtle and influential tenets of wisdom of the rishis’, hence ensuring the maintenance of public belief in Oriental Studies.
